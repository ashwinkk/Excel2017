About Us

Excel, the nation’s second and South India’s first ever fest of its kind started in 2001 by the students of Govt. Model Engineering College with a motto, Inspire, Innovate, Engineer. Over the years, Excel has grown exponentially, consistently playing host to some of the most talented students, the most illustrious speakers and the most reputed companies. Now gearing towards its 18th edition, Excel continues to excite the participants with its wide array of technical and non-technical events and with a promise to live upto its motto.
